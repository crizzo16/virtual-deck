  • Button Color Guide

    Green buttons draw cards from your deck.

    Red buttons only work if you select a card from your hand.

    Blue buttons deal with cards from your discard pile.

    KO card button also deals with your discard pile, but it's a different color so you don't accidentally hit it.

    Purple buttons deal with cards in the HQ.

    Orange buttons add or remove Heroes for your specific game.
